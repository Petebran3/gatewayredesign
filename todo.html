New york is a city for all people of different cultures and backgrounds. What ever you can think of New York has it. New York has 16 Professional Sport teams, more than 20,000 restaurants, and countless places where you can find some great entertainment.

Highly Recommended Places


1. Visit a New York Knicks Basketball Game.

  • Location: 4 Pennsylvania Plaza, New York, NY 10001
  • Cost/Ticket sales: Tickets range from $40.00 to $9,000.00 dollars, depending on where you wanna sit.
  • Age Group: There is no age limit to attend a Knicks game, but kids under two get in for free, with a guardian.
  • Description: Even though the team isn't that good, a Knicks game is always a 10/10 experience. They have one of the best fan bases in sports. They're games are hosted at the historical Madison Square Garden Arena. One of the most famous areans in the United States. So if you never been to a NBA game. A Knicks game would be a great experience.




2. Visit the Dhamaka Restaurant.

  • Location: 119 Delancey St, New York, NY 10002
  • Cost: Prices range from $3.00-$190.00 dollars.
  • Age Group: There is no age limit to eat at the restaurant.
  • Decription: The Indian cuisine restaurant opened in 2021 and took New York by storm. Ranked the Number one restaurant in New York City in 2021 during covid. Their most famous item on the menu is the "Rajasthani Khargosh" which is a whole rabbit for $190.00 dollars. It serves 3-4 people and is so popular it's first come, first served, and only can be ordered once.

3. The New York City Broadway Week.

  • Location: Times Square New York City/ Midtown Manhattan, New York
  • Cost/Ticket sales: Prices range from $20.00-$175.00 dollars.
  • Age Group: There is no age limit.
  • Description: The most famous broadway shows they have is Aladdin, The Lion King, The Phantom of the Opera, and last but not least Hamilton, which was voted the number one broadway show in New York City. Broadway Week starts January 18th to February 13. During that time you can get two for one ticket prices for some of New York's best shows.

4. Flight Club NYC.
  • Location: 812 Broadway, New York, NY
  • Cost/Price Range: Prices range from $25.00-$100,000 dollars.
  • Age Group: There is no age limit for flight club, but shoes sizes start at a size 1-10 for youth, size 3-18 for men, and size 4-17.5 for women.
  • Description: Flight club is one of the top shoe stores in the United States. They have brands such as Nike, Adidas, Jordans, Asics, Under Armour, Reebok, Vans, Converse, Puma, and New Balance. Whatever shoe you're looking for in those brands they have it.
